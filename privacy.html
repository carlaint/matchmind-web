Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 10, 2026

Your data is yours. We don't sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it to train AI models. We only collect what's needed to run the app.

What we collect

When you create an account and use MatchMind AI, we store:

  • Your name, email address, and username
  • A securely hashed version of your password (we cannot see your actual password)
  • Your preferred format (singles or doubles)
  • Match logs, scores, and results you enter
  • Player names, notes, and scouting data you record
  • Voice note transcriptions (text only — see below)
  • Saved courts and locations
  • AI-generated player summaries and strategies

Voice recordings

When you use the voice note feature, your speech is converted to text directly on your iPhone using Apple's built-in speech recognition. Audio is never recorded, stored, or transmitted to our servers — only the transcribed text is saved.

How your data is stored

Your data is stored on our backend server, which is hosted on Render. All communication between the app and our server uses HTTPS encryption. Each user's data is isolated — you can only see your own players, matches, and notes.

Backups and data recovery

Pro users: We maintain server-side backups of your data. If you lose access or accidentally delete something, you can email us to request data recovery.

Free users: We do not maintain server-side backups. If your data is lost, we cannot recover it. You can export your data as a JSON file anytime from the Account tab as a self-service backup.

Who can access your data

Only you can see your data through the app. As the operator of the service, we have technical access to the database, but we will only access your data when you specifically request it (for example, if a Pro user asks us to recover lost data). We do not read, review, or share your match notes or player data for any other purpose.

AI-generated summaries

When you request AI insights, the text of your notes is sent securely to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a summary or strategy. During this process:

  • Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections
  • Anthropic does not use API inputs to train their models
  • The generated insights are stored on our server so you can view them again later

Data security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt, an industry-standard algorithm designed to resist brute-force attacks. All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption.

Your rights

You can:

  • Edit your profile and password anytime from the Account tab
  • Export all your data as a JSON file from the Account tab
  • Request account deletion by tapping "Delete Account" in the app or emailing us

Third-party services

MatchMind AI uses these services to operate:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes note text for AI-generated insights
  • Render — hosts our backend server and database
  • Apple Speech Recognition — transcribes voice notes on your device

We do not use advertising networks, third-party analytics, trackers, or social media SDKs.

AI-generated content disclaimer

Player summaries, strategies, and other AI-generated insights are produced by artificial intelligence based on the match logs and notes you provide. These are intended as informational tools to support your preparation and should not be relied upon as guarantees of match outcomes. AI-generated content may contain inaccuracies. Use your own judgment when making decisions on court.

Children's privacy

MatchMind AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes, we'll notify users through the app. Minor clarifications may be made without notice.
